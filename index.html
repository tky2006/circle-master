📖 How to Play

⭕ Circle Master
Cambridge Mathematics · Geometry
Master the world of circles! Sort circle parts, calculate measurements, and estimate dimensions across 10 exciting rounds. Earn up to 3 stars!
🔄 Circle Sorter · Rounds 1–3
Drag circle part diagrams (radius, chord, arc…) to their matching definition bins. No labels — identify by shape!
🔢 Circle Calculator · Rounds 4–6
Spin the interactive circle diagram, read the given values, then type your calculated answer. C, A, arc length, sector area.
🎯 Circle Estimator · Rounds 7–9
A circle appears — drag the slider to estimate its radius, diameter, or circumference by eye.
